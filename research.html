JSS Science and Technology University

Research

JSS STU University, rooted in the esteemed legacy of SJCE, Mysuru, is fervently committed to advancing Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, and Management through cutting-edge research and innovation. Recognized for its dedication to tackling contemporary challenges, the university is a hub for impactful academic research and technological advancements. Faculty members spearhead pioneering research endeavors, pushing the boundaries of knowledge in their respective fields. Concurrently, graduate students actively contribute to research through coursework and dissertations, while undergraduates, whether in their coursework or engaged in internships and research fellowships, are encouraged to participate in the institution's culture of innovation. Renowned for its academic excellence in professional programs and a holistic approach, JSS STU University stands out as a premier destination for those seeking to make significant contributions to the realms of research and innovation in Science and Engineering.
