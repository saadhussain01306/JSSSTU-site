Campus Facilities

Campus offers a well-equipped infrastructure with cutting-edge resources to help students and teachers in their academic and research activities.

Classrooms

The college has separate laboratories for basic science, All the laboratories are adequately equipped as per the University norms.

Central Library

The College Library is the hub of learning with a rich collection of books, e-books, standard reference sources and other audio-visual material.

The institution features three auditoriums, each with the required audio-visual equipment.

Athletics & Fitness

Physical education-is an integral part of the educational programme designed to promote the optimum development of an individual.

Separate hostel facilities are provided for Boys and Girls in the college campus. It has adequate high security and good discipline.

With a lush green outfield and a well-maintained pitch, the ground provides an excellent setting for both local and regional cricket matches.

Having a bank and ATM inside a university campus can be highly convenient for students, faculty, and staff.

Digital Library

The digital hub of the library has access to a large number of e-books, e-journals, and online databases along with access to various e-resources across the library.
