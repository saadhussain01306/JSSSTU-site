LINUX CAMPUS CLUB (LCC-JSSSTU)

Faculty mentor: Dr. Prasanna B T, Associate Prof. Dept of CSE

The only Technical Club of Computer Science and Engineering Department, founded in 2004, which focuses on conducting various Technical Events throughout the year including Coding Contests, Technical Bootcamps and Sessions, providing students hands-on experience on the various Cutting Edge Technologies, all designed to upskill the Technical skills of the students. The club is also responsible for conducting annual technical events FOSSCAMP and FOSSBYTES and also annual fun event FUNIX.

IEEE-SJCE

Faculty mentor:Dr Veena M G ,Prof. Dept of ECE

IEEE-SJCE is the student branch of the well-known International organization the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) which engages in a range of activities to foster technical knowledge, leadership skills, and professional development among students. The club is responsible to organize technical workshops, seminars, and competitions, host conferences for research dissemination, conduct community outreach programs and publish technical journals.

COMPUTER SOCIETY OF INDIA (CSI-JSSSTU)

Faculty mentor:Dr.S P Shiva Prakash.,Prof. Dept of ISE

CSI JSSSTU, your gateway to the world of technology and innovation. As a prominent technical club at our college, we offer an exciting array of year-round events and activities that cater to tech enthusiasts. Our journey begins with "Enigma," our annual introductory fun event. Enigma sets the tone for the academic year by providing a platform for students to connect, engage in team-building, and ignite their passion for technology. The highlight of our calendar is "Technologix," our flagship annual technical event. Technologix showcases the zenith of technological prowess through a diverse range of competitions, hackathons, tech talks, and workshops. It's where innovation meets implementation.

BUREAU CLUB

Faculty mentor: Dr Sidram M H Department of EEE.

Bureau Club at SJCE is the first Civil Services aspirants club in a technical institute in Karnataka. It provides a common platform for aspiring candidates to prepare for the tough Civil Services exam. The club aims to motivate and assist students in preparing for UPSC examination while also focusing on their overall development and building confidence.

SAHAS ADVENTURE CLUBD

Sahas is the only adventure club of JSS STU Mysore, which enables students of our college to participate in adventure sports and embrace the challenges of outdoor activities. The club conducts wide range of programs, including Adventure Junkyard, one of the most awaited events during Jayciana, two-day treks, one-day treks, rock climbing, rappelling, rafting, basic mountaineering course, marathon, cyclothon and five-day aqua and terrestrial camps. Sahas aims to motivate students to step out of their comfort zones, develop resilience and appreciation for nature. The club strives to build strong individuals and educate students about the importance and beauty of the natural world.
